about fern

Hey, I'm fern-d3v — a web development student documenting my journey into full-stack development here on console.log.


Naturally, as a nerdy person (I love ttrpg, fantasy reading, video games), I enjoy all things tech. One of my more niche enjoyments is learning new pathways of tech. Web Development just felt like the right choice for me to make when reassessing my career. It has multiple unique languages to learn as well as a whole new set of best practices (languages and rules are wonderful arent they?) to learn, which makes it interesting enough that my brain won't get bored of it.


This blog is where I track my progress, share my struggles, and celebrate small wins. I'm building console.log as a record of what it really looks like to learn web development from scratch. Through this I will learn how to become a Full-Stack Web Developer!
